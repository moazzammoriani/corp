questions?

what formats do you accept?

mp4, mkv, mcap, rosbag (1 & 2), hdf5, rlds/tfds, lerobot datasets, and raw sensor dumps. if it's weird, send it anyway — we like weird.

how fast is turnaround?

pilots come back within 3–5 days. at scale we agree an sla per batch — most clients run on a weekly delivery cadence.

how do you handle quality?

every label passes at least two annotators plus automated consistency checks. we report inter-annotator agreement with every delivery, and disputed frames escalate to senior reviewers.

is my data safe?

yes. encrypted in transit and at rest, annotators work in locked-down environments with no export, and we sign ndas before seeing a single frame. on-prem and vpc deployments available.

do you use your own tooling?

we run a custom stack built for multi-camera, multi-sensor episodes — synced video, 3d skeleton overlays, and timeline segmentation in one view. we can also work inside your tooling.

can you handle pii in egocentric footage?

yes — faces, screens, license plates, and bystander audio can be blurred or redacted as a pre-processing pass before annotation begins.

how does pricing work?

per labelled unit (frame, episode, or hour of footage) depending on task complexity. pilots are free — you'll get an exact quote with your pilot results.
